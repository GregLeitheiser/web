In short, that what we're going to find out. Servant's Code is a Benedict Option community with a mission to develop high quality software to support the mission of the Catholic Church. In his work "The Benedict Option", Rod Dreher outlines a program for a new form of lay religious life focused on vigorously living the Christian life in close community with fellow Christians. From this community, he posits that a well lived Christian life can breathe life into its surrounding culture.


But a community cannot exist without work and a mission to give it focus and direction. Greg, our head servant, has a background in software design and development. Surveying the state of software supporting the Catholic Church today, he found that his talents could be used to serve through the development of modern systems to support parishes in the Dallas Diocese.


On this basis, Servant's Code was founded. We are a non-profit, open-source software community specializing in helping parishes minister well and operate efficiently.


Stay tuned, we're going to do great things!
